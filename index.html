2019 created a stressful flood, which inundated so many houses, stores and factories in kerala
It was a mind boggling situation. It devastated so many towns and cities. In somany areas, electricity was washed away by the tremendous power of water. Somany were isolated in different areas and they find it difficult to contact others. Lives were lost due to lack of communication. In this hard time IEDC and IEEE MACE SB decided to extend our helping hands to the flood affected areas. We tried to create humanity in technology. We developed and provided solar powered mobile charging units to flood affected areas of Wayanad and Nilambur. It was developed and fabricated at MACE fablab and delivered the same to the required areas. This incident made IEDC remarkable.

About FabLab


Fabrication Laboratory (FabLab) in MACE is a platform for creative and innovative minds. Almost anything can be fabricated here. This is a simply amazing space to develop the designs and prototypes. This plays a major role in fabrication and implementation of projects. Fablab helps us to create mechanical structures, electronics circuits and a user interface. 3D printing and model making is easy with fablab. 3D Printer, Laser cutter , Vinyl cutter , Reflow oven, and workbench is available here. 3D creates a three dimensional model of any shape. Laser cutter is the technology which uses a laser to slice materials. Vinyl cutter is a computer controlled machine used for trimming and to cut out shapes and letters from sheets of thin self adhesive plastic. Also for reflow soldering of surface mount electronic components to printed circuit is possible in fablab through Reflow Oven. In short, fablab helps to boost startups and paves a good way for IEDC.
